Let’s review your home’s accessibility features

Adding photos of your accessibility features helps showcase your space to the 70,000+ guests with mobility needs who search on Airbnb every month.

What you’ll do

  • Step 1

    Go through your space and look for accessibility features. Bring a tape measure if you have one.

  • Step 2

    Select the features you have and add photos of them.

  • Step 3

    Preview how these photos will appear in your listing’s new Accessibility section.
